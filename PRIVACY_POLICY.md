# Fairway Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

Fairway is designed to keep golf rollup scoring simple and private.

## What Fairway collects

Fairway does not require account creation and does not ask users to submit personal information to a remote server as part of the core app experience.

The app stores round information locally on the device, such as:

- round names
- player names entered by the user
- handicaps entered by the user
- group setup
- tee selections
- score entries
- saved round history

## How data is used

This information is used only to provide rollup setup, score logging, leaderboard calculation, and saved round history inside the app.

## Data sharing

Fairway does not sell user data.

Fairway does not share user-entered round data with third parties as part of the core app experience.

## Data retention

Saved rounds are kept on the user’s device until the user removes the app or deletes the saved data inside the app.

## Children’s privacy

Fairway is not directed specifically at children.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the Fairway support page.
